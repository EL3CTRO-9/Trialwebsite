01 — About

The Journey So Far

I'm a cybersecurity professional with a passion for building secure systems and exploring the mathematical foundations that underpin them. Currently serving as Assistant Manager of Information Security, I bridge the gap between technical implementation and strategic security planning.

My path has taken me through red teaming, SIEM architecture, policy development, and beyond. With 9 GIAC certifications and a SANS graduate degree, I've built a foundation that spans offensive and defensive security.

But security is just one chapter. My curiosity extends to mathematics, where I find the same elegance and precision that drew me to cybersecurity in the first place. Everything connects, everything cycles back — that's the philosophy that drives me forward.
